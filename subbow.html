Subbow

Unreal Engine 4, Oculus Quest

A VR shooter in which the player is stuck in a sinking submarine, armed with only a bow & toilet plunger. Pipes are bursting, so you best plug them to stop the water level from rising & survive as long as you can.

Features

My responsibilites included creating a variable rising water rate, randomized pipe-bursting event system, underwater post-processing effects, & scene lighting.

Future Improvements

  • Improve lighting & material quality to compensate for hardware limitations on Quest (possibly port to other hardware?)
  • More explicit HUD elements to indicate water level
  • More detailed water spray effects
